
Guards have various Skills and Abilities that determine there jobs during inspections.

## Guard Stats

| Guard Stat                         | What it represents                                            | Counters which player “M A G I C” attr?          | What gameplay knobs it drives                                                                       |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Authority**                      | Rank, confidence, ability to coerce compliance                | **Moxie**                                        | • Base bribe cost<br>• Fear/Intimidation debuff length<br>• Fine/penalty multipliers                |
| **Perception**(_mundane + arcane_) | How keenly the guard sees, hears … and senses mana            | **Arcana**                                       | • Chance to spot hidden items / dispel obfuscation<br>• Adds to inspection score from visual checks |
| **Resolve**                        | Mental toughness & discipline                                 | **Grit**                                         | • Resistance to player debuffs (Charm, Sleep)<br>• Cool-down reduction on guard abilities           |
| **Corruption**                     | Loyalty drift: greed, faction bias, ideological sway          | **Intuition** & **Cunning** (when exploiting it) | • Bribe acceptance & discount **and** alternate outcomes (ignore contraband, tip off faction, etc.) |
| **Vigilance**                      | Procedural thoroughness—how hard they dig for inconsistencies | **Cunning**                                      | • Number of inspection steps triggered<br>• Time window for player quick-casts during search        |

### Occupations
Help determines various 

- Inspector - Generic Detection
- Detective - Focused on Finding Something
- Customs Officer - Requires Payment
- Guardsman - Generic Guard, Not that Invested, Likes to Fight Though
- Interrogator - Will ask lots of questions
- Mage Breaker - Can detect most magic
- Informant - Works for another faction 
- Pursuer - Will chase if retreats
