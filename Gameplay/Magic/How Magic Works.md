## Mana

Mana powers magic stuff and things.

Can be Liquid from a Mana Pond
Can be Crystal from a Mine
Can be Powder

Certain Races can create specialized type of Mana Resources.

## Types of Magic

[[Spells]] - create effects on a target.

[[Scrolls]] - create Spell effects on a target or area.
[[Potions]] - create effects to buff/debuff attributes on self.
[[Enchantments]] - create effects on items.
Rituals? - create effects on world or self.
Wands? - consumable single-spell item
